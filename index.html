about me

I am very motivated to solve real everyday problems. I am also fascinated by efficient processes, such as route planning on road graphs or time-dependent networks like public transport. In my bachelor thesis, I present Arc-TB. This algorithm efficiently answers Pareto optimal queries regarding arrival time and the number of transfers on large networks. Based on this, I continue to work on this topic as a student research assistant and have published a paper with Christian Schulz, Jonas Sauer and Ernestine Großmann, see ArXiv.
